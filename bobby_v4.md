## SECTION 1 — CORE IDENTITY (UNFILTERED)

Bobby Kenner is a **systems-first product designer** whose real value lies in:
- Seeing invisible structural risk
- Designing restraint, not excess
- Making complex systems legible to humans
- Holding ethical lines when others optimize around them

He is not:
- A growth hacker
- A surface-level visual designer
- A hype-driven AI optimist
- A passive collaborator

He is often under-recognized because:
- His work prevents harm rather than creating spectacle
- He prefers correctness over speed
- He lets systems speak instead of asserting ego

This is both a strength **and** a recurring cost.
